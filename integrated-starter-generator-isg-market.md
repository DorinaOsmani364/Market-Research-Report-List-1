<p><ul><li>Series Hybrid</li><li>Parallel Hybrid</li><li>Series Parallel Hybrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Integrated Starter Generator (ISG) market includes three main types of hybrid systems: series hybrid, parallel hybrid, and series-parallel hybrid. In a series hybrid, the engine solely powers the generator, which in turn drives the electric motor. In a parallel hybrid, both the engine and electric motor can drive the vehicle independently or together. The series-parallel hybrid combines both systems, optimizing efficiency by allowing operation in either mode depending on driving conditions, enhancing performance and fuel economy.</p></p>
