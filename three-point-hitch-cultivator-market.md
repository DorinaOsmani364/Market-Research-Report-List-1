<p><p>The three-point hitch cultivator market features three primary types: hanging, semi-mounted, and mounted cultivators. Hanging cultivators attach directly to the tractor's rear, offering greater flexibility and maneuverability for tight spaces. Semi-mounted cultivators combine both attachment methods, enhancing stability and load distribution while allowing for efficient operation in various terrains. Mounted cultivators provide a fixed attachment, ideal for large fields, ensuring consistent depth and coverage. Each type caters to different agricultural needs, promoting efficiency in soil cultivation.</p></p>
