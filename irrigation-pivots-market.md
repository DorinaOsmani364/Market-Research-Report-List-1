<p><p>Irrigation pivots can be categorized into two main market types: mobile and fixed. Mobile pivots are designed for flexibility, allowing farmers to relocate them easily to different fields, enhancing efficiency for diverse crops. In contrast, fixed pivots are stationary installations that provide consistent coverage for specific areas, maximizing water usage over time. Both types serve unique agricultural needs, with mobile pivots catering to varied cropping practices and fixed pivots optimizing resource management within established boundaries.</p></p>
