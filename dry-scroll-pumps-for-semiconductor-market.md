<p><p>Dry scroll pumps are pivotal in the semiconductor industry for maintaining vacuum environments essential for fabrication processes. Static vortex pumps use a fixed design to create a vortex motion that efficiently moves gases, ideal for high-flow applications. In contrast, moving vortex pumps feature rotating components that generate a dynamic vortex, enabling better performance in varying pressure conditions. Both types offer contamination-free operation, crucial for semiconductor manufacturing, and provide high reliability and energy efficiency, catering to the stringent demands of this market.</p></p>
