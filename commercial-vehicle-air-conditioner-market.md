<p><ul><li>Reciprocating Type</li><li>Rotary Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial vehicle air conditioner market features two main types: reciprocating and rotary systems. Reciprocating AC units use pistons to compress refrigerant, delivering high cooling capacity and efficiency, making them suitable for larger vehicles. In contrast, rotary air conditioners employ rotating components to compress refrigerant, offering a more compact design, quieter operation, and lower vibrations, which are ideal for smaller vehicles and applications with space constraints. Both types cater to diverse commercial vehicle needs, focusing on performance and reliability.</p></p>
