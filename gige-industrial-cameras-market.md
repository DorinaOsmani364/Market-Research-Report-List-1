<p><p>The GigE Industrial Cameras market primarily features two sensor types: CCD and CMOS. CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) cameras offer superior image quality and low noise levels, making them ideal for applications requiring high precision and sensitivity, particularly in low-light conditions. Conversely, CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) cameras are known for their faster frame rates, lower power consumption, and integrated functionalities, making them suitable for high-speed imaging and cost-sensitive applications. Together, these technologies drive innovation and cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
