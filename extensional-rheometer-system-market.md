<p><p>The Extensional Rheometer System Market comprises various types, including Dynamic Type and Capillary Type, among others. Dynamic Type rheometers measure the material's response under oscillatory shear, providing insights into its viscoelastic properties. Capillary Type rheometers, on the other hand, assess flow behavior by forcing materials through a narrow channel, focusing on extensional viscosity. Other types may include specialized instruments tailored for specific applications or research, catering to diverse industries such as polymers, food, and pharmaceuticals aiming for optimal material performance.</p></p>
