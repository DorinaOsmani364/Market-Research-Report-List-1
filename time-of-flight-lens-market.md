FOV < 50° lenses are typically used for applications requiring high precision at short distances, like industrial automation. FOV 50°-100° lenses balance precision and coverage, suitable for robotics and mobile devices. FOV > 100° lenses offer expansive coverage, ideal for surveillance, automotive, and augmented reality applications, enabling greater scene capture and depth perception.</p></p>
