<p><ul><li>Press Molding</li><li>Casting Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Quartz Facade market can be divided into two key types: Press Molding and Casting Molding. Press Molding involves applying heat and pressure to a mixture of quartz and resin, creating dense, durable panels ideal for high-impact applications. In contrast, Casting Molding involves pouring liquid resin mixed with quartz into molds, allowing for intricate designs and textures. Both methods cater to diverse architectural needs, offering aesthetic versatility and functional performance in constructing buildings and interior spaces.</p></p>
