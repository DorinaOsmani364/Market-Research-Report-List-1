<p><strong>The Industrial Water Deionizers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two-bed Deionization</li><li>Mixed-bed Deionization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial water deionizers market features two primary types: two-bed deionization and mixed-bed deionization. Two-bed deionization utilizes separate columns for cation and anion exchange resins, effectively removing ion contaminants but requiring periodic regeneration. In contrast, mixed-bed deionization combines both cation and anion resins in a single vessel, achieving higher purity levels in a compact design. While two-bed systems are simpler and more cost-effective, mixed-bed units offer superior water quality, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
