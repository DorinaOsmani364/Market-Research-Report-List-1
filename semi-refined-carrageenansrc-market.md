<p><strong>The Semi-Refined Carrageenan（SRC） Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Kappa Carrageenan</li><li>Iota Carrageenan</li><li>Lambda Carrageenan</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semi-refined carrageenan (SRC) is derived from red seaweed and is commonly used as a thickening and gelling agent in food and other industries. The market is categorized into three main types: Kappa carrageenan, which forms strong gels in the presence of potassium ions; Iota carrageenan, known for its gel strength and elasticity, particularly with calcium ions; and Lambda carrageenan, which thickens but does not gel effectively. Each type has unique properties that cater to specific applications in food, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
