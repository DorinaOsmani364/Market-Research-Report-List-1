<p><p>The Non-Mechanical Surface Treatment Equipment market includes technologies like Plasma Treatment and Corona Treatment, which enhance surface properties without physical abrasion. Plasma Treatment utilizes ionized gases to modify surfaces, improving adhesion, wettability, and chemical resistance for various materials. Conversely, Corona Treatment employs high-voltage electrical discharges to create reactive species on surfaces, also aimed at enhancing adhesion and inkability. Both methods are widely used in industries such as packaging, automotive, and electronics to optimize product performance and quality.</p></p>
